Privacy policy

At Medico Plus, our foremost commitment is to safeguard your privacy and guarantee the security of your personal information. Our data production practices are meticulously crafted to prioritize the confidentiality of your data, and we want to assure you that we handle your information with the utmost care. Specifically, we want to emphasize that your health card data is treated with the highest level of security, and under no circumstances is it shared with any third party.

When it comes to data collection, we adhere to a principle of minimalism, ensuring that we only collect the information necessary to provide you with our services and enhance your overall experience. Your consent is of paramount importance to us, and we ensure that we obtain explicit permission before collecting any personal information. You retain control over your data, with the ability to update preferences or opt-out at any time.

Our security measures are state-of-the-art, incorporating robust encryption and access controls to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Access to your personal information is restricted to authorized personnel with a legitimate need, and we conduct regular audits to ensure compliance with our stringent privacy standards.

Transparent communication is integral to our approach. Any changes to our privacy practices are communicated promptly, allowing you the opportunity to review and understand modifications. We strictly adhere to data protection laws and regulations, ensuring that our practices align with the highest standards of privacy and security.

At Medico Plus, we recognize the sensitive nature of health card data, and you can trust that your information is handled with the utmost diligence and respect for your privacy.
